A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, typically composed of several sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It serves to organize and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought. Each paragraph should ideally develop a single point or idea, with sentences working together to support a controlling idea
